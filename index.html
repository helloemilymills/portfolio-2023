Welcome to my maker space

I'm a Product Designer, hobby sewist, Communication Design student, and dabbler in web development. What ties all that together? I love learning how things are made.

As we move into the latter half of 2023, it's a goal of mine to share more of what I create. As someone with perfectionist tendancies, I often think my work needs more polish before it's ready to be shared. But sharing imperfect work is better than not sharing – and not growing – at all.
